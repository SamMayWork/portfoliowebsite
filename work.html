Argos - Responsible Colleague

October 2017 - Present

In this multi-talented role, I was challenged to provide a high level of customer service while also undertaking and getting involved with other areas of a retail store such a managing stock and overseeing deliveries into the business.

My main responsilbility in this role was helping customers to find the product right for them, processing their orders and working with the till system. If a particular product wasn't in stock it would also be my challenge to find a way of getting a product to a customer whether this be organising a delivery or working with them to find an alternative suitable product.

This role also included oppurtunities to go beyond my job role, allowing me to manage stock and oversee deliveries into the business. I also pushed myself and performed addtional duties in this role including basic cash office duties such as cashing up tills at the end of the shift.

Skills

Volunteering

Code Club - Volunteer

October 2018 - May 2019

During my time volunteering I was presented with many challenges which allowed me to further develop skills which I had been using throughout University and work.

This role involved running a Code Club event in a local school, teaching pupils between the ages of 9 and 11 the basics of computer programming.

The challenge of this role was simplifying difficult to understand concepts to a level which could be understood by primary aged pupils while also trying to inspire them to experiment with code and have fun.

Working with primary pupils trying to inspire them to experiment with code was the most rewarding part of this experience, along with the skills which I developed.

Skills

  • Simplifying Complex Ideas
  • Finding ways to make content engaging
  • Changing content to suit needs of the students
